Kia Ora!

Welcome to Ako Maori - Your introduction to the wonderful Maori culture.


'Ako Maori' translates to "Learning Maori" in the aformentioned language. This is your virtual trainer to both the heart and the soul of Maori culture. Our mission here is to provide and facilitate a learning service in order to broaden and expand your knowledge of the Maori culture. Through the use of different games we see to provide a blend of educational and excitement to learning the culture, language and heart of the Maori people.


Discover the Maori Language:

Begin your studious journey by delving into the depths of the Maori language. As mentioned above the Maori Word Match Game is designed to make learning the language and developing key skills fun and enjoyable in order to ensure that it sticks better with you as a learner.
